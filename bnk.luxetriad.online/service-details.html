The Imperial Premium-BANK Auto Loan!

Get your wheels on the road … with the Imperial Premium-BANK Limited Auto Loan.


The Imperial Premium-BANK Limited Auto loan offers you short to medium term loans for the purchase of brand new vehicles with the convenience of spreading repayments over a period of time. This is available to both salaried customers and business owners. It enables customer purchase vehicles from auto dealers who have been prequalified and have agreed an SLA with the bank

Features
  • Borrow $50,000.00 to $150,000.00
  • Payment over 12 to 48 months
  • Contribute as low as 20% towards the purchase.
  • Comprehensive Insurance on the car for the entire loan period
  • No security required
  • Competitive pricing and no hidden charges.
Benefits
  • Installation of free tracking device for auto valued at $300, 000 and above(Free for cars above $3milion otherwise, $50,000.00)
  • Buy a car of your choice from our list of acceptable brands and spread payment over a convenient period
  • We have reliable dealers accredited to ensure you buy from a safe source.
  • Convenient insurance payment included in the loan repayment amount
  • No residual cost at end of loan tenure
